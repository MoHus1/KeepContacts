How to use...

  • Enter information in the "Add A New Contact" from, then submit the from to find the new contact in the list of contacts
  • Double click on the edit icon in a cell to edit the cell's content. When you double click, you will be prompted to enter the new content of the cell. Do so then press "Edit Contact" to see the edited contact in the table
  • You can filter your contacts by first name, last name, email, or phone number. To do so, just enter the value you want into the appropiate field of the "Search Contacts By..." form and see the table shrink in real-time.
  • If you leave a field blank when filling in the "Add Contact" form, a value of "N/A" (Not Applicable) will automatically be applied to that field.
  • Check the "About" section to learn more
